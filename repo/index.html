HAVE QUESTIONS?

FAQ.

Do I need to book in advance for drop-in? +
While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking your pass online to guarantee entry, as we frequently reach capacity on weekends.
What age are the kids clinics for? +
Our weekend clinics are separated by age groups: 6-8, 9-11, and 12-14. We adjust the hoop height and basketball sizes accordingly.
Can we bring outside food for birthday parties? +
Yes! You are welcome to bring your own cake and snacks for the party room. We also offer catering add-ons if you prefer us to handle the pizza and drinks.
Are basketballs provided? +
We provide basketballs for all clinics and parties. For drop-in play or court rentals, we ask that you bring your own, though we do have limited rentals available at the front desk.
